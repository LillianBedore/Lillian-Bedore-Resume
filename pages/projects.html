Inspiritus

Inspiritus, formerly Lutheran Services of Georgia and Lutheran Services in Tennessee, is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization that provides programs and services in Georgia, Tennessee and Alabama, in the areas of refugee and immigrant services, children and family services, disaster relief and disability host home services.

  • Restored and helped to organize a local refugee run coffee shop, Refuge Coffee Co.
  • Interacted with local peoples to better understand how to be an ally and advocate for change
  • Organized free clothes closet for summer, and made care packages for the community

The Welcome Co-Op

The Welcome Co-op is Georgia's trusted advocate for new refugees, helping to secure housing and ensure a smooth and seamless transition for the state's newest and ever-resilient residents.

  • Swicthed out winter stock of clothes at the free clothing closet
  • Organized warehouse and implmented system for future donations
  • Interacted with locals to learn about their journey and become more aware of individual journeys
